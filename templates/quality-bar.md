# Quality Bar

Use this optional file only when quality criteria are repeatedly forgotten or applied inconsistently.

## General criteria

- The output satisfies the accepted work order.
- Scope is controlled; non-goals were not touched.
- The actor stayed inside the authority envelope; technical access was not treated as authorization.
- Sources and the canonical revision were fresh enough for the task.
- Parallel work preserved single-writer ownership or used isolated, non-overlapping write lanes.
- External side effects are complete, reviewable, and accurately reported.
- Edge states are considered where relevant.
- Accessibility is checked in actual use, not only in markup.
- Public claims are supported by evidence.
- Validation evidence is recorded as `Passed`, `Failed`, or `Not run`.
- State documents remain accurate after the change.
- The continuity checkpoint is sufficient to resume on the correct surface, host, and revision.
